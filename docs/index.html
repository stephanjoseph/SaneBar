One Click. Instant Clarity.

Everything left of the / disappears. Everything right stays visible. That's it.

Notch-Friendly Recovery

Recover hidden icons on MacBook Pros by tightening the gap between items. Perfect for the "notch" era where screen real estate is at a premium.

Keyboard-First Workflow

Assign global hotkeys to individual menu bar items. Launch Zoom, toggle Wi-Fi, or check battery without ever lifting your hands from the keys.

0% Telemetry. 100% Privacy.

SaneBar has no network permissions. No analytics, no "phone home," no tracking. Just a clean menu bar and peace of mind.
